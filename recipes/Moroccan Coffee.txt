Moroccan Coffee

Ingredients:

2 cups strong brewed coffee
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger (optional)
2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
1-2 teaspoons orange blossom water (optional, for a floral note)
Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)
Instructions:

Prepare the Coffee:

Brew 2 cups of strong coffee. You can use a drip coffee maker, French press, or any method you prefer.
Add Spices:

While the coffee is still hot, stir in the ground cinnamon, ground cardamom, and ground ginger (if using).
Sweeten:

Add sugar to the coffee while it is still warm, stirring until fully dissolved. Adjust the amount of sugar to your taste preference.
Add Orange Blossom Water (Optional):

For an extra layer of flavor, stir in 1-2 teaspoons of orange blossom water. This ingredient is optional but adds a distinct, aromatic quality to the coffee.
Serve:

Pour the spiced coffee into cups.
Garnish (Optional):

Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a refreshing touch.
Enjoy:

Moroccan coffee is typically enjoyed as a warm and comforting beverage, perfect for sipping and savoring.
