Italian Cream Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
1/4 cup Italian cream liqueur (such as Amaretto or Baileys Irish Cream) or a non-alcoholic coffee cream liqueur substitute
1/4 cup milk or half-and-half
2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
Ice cubes
Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)
Cocoa powder or shaved chocolate (optional, for garnish)
Instructions:

Prepare the Coffee:

Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool to room temperature. For an even cooler drink, chill it in the refrigerator.
Mix the Cream:

In a small bowl, mix the Italian cream liqueur (or non-alcoholic substitute) with the milk or half-and-half.
Sweeten the Coffee:

Stir the sugar into the cooled coffee until it is fully dissolved.
Combine Ingredients:

In a large glass, add ice cubes to fill about half of the glass.
Pour the sweetened coffee over the ice.
Add the cream mixture and stir gently to combine.
Garnish (Optional):

Top with a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
Sprinkle with cocoa powder or shaved chocolate for an extra touch.
Serve:

Enjoy immediately as a refreshing and indulgent iced coffee drink.
